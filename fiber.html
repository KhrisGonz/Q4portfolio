Fiber Based Systems

High-speed data transmission using light signals through fiber-optic cables.

Description

Fiber optics transmit data as pulses of light through strands of glass or plastic. They offer faster speeds and greater bandwidth compared to copper.

Function

Fiber systems are widely used for high-speed internet, long-distance communication, and backbone infrastructure in telecom networks.
